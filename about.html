About Me

I am an AI & Data Science undergraduate currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Nehru Institute of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore, affiliated with Anna University, with a CGPA of 7.9. I have a strong academic and practical foundation in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Generative AI, Data Analytics, and Cybersecurity fundamentals, and I am passionate about building intelligent, data-driven, and secure technology solutions.

Alongside AI and data science, I have developed a strong interest in cybersecurity and networking. I have practical exposure to web application penetration testing, vulnerability assessment (VAPT), and OWASP Top 10 security risks. I have gained valuable hands-on and industry-oriented experience through multiple internships and virtual training programs. In addition, I have completed AI/ML, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Networking internships under organizations such as IBM SkillsBuild, Edunet Foundation, ApexPlanet, YBI Foundation, and Juniper Networks, which strengthened both my technical knowledge and analytical thinking.


My project experience includes developing a Smart Environment Monitoring System using sensors and machine learning, a Face and Emotion Recognition system using computer vision techniques, a Hand Gesture-Based Control system for real-time interaction, Web Application Penetration Testing using DVWA, and Network Topology Simulation using Cisco Packet Tracer. These projects helped me bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and practical implementation.


I also have strong exposure to Generative AI and Prompt Engineering, including an understanding of large language models, prompt structuring, AI agents, and responsible AI practices. Through professional certifications from IBM, AWS, and Google Cloud, I have built a solid foundation in Generative AI concepts and real-world AI applications, which I actively integrate with my data science and cybersecurity learning.


Overall, I am a motivated, detail-oriented learner who enjoys solving complex problems at the intersection of AI, data, security, and automation. I am eager to contribute to AI engineering, data science, cybersecurity, or SOC-oriented roles, where I can continue learning, building impactful solutions, and growing as a technology professional.
